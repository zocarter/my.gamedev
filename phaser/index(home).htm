The Next Step

It is our job to maintain the stability of our ecosystem and keep the environment safe. The best way to do so
is to end bad habits such as littering. An easy way to help the environment without drastically changing one's lifestyle
is to throw trash in its proper bins. Meaning to recycle plastic and compost your food. With just a little research on
how to properly throw your trash away one will become a model civilian in our society and hopefully be able to pass their
teachings to their friends and family. Decreasing the amount of trash in our streets. Watching what we throw away
can help not only us but the wildlife around us. If people stand by together and work hard to watch what they throw
away it would help increase animal safety. But in the meantime as people learn how to correct their ways. If someone sees
an animal in danger or stuck in trash you should call ASPCA or 911 in order to maintain your safety and the animals.
